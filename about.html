About Me

I am a senior at the University of Michigan studying UX design. I am a motivated student studying the relationship between technology and people, using my technical skills to build interactive systems through understanding user needs, generating prototypes, and evaluating designs. I am passionate about giving users satisfying interactions with products. In my free time I am tutoring children in Ann Arbor and teaching them how to cook healthy meals. I love sushi, traveling, and karaoke.

What I'm Working on Now

I am currently working on adding a voice user interface to the University of Michigan app. This involves researching, testing, and prototyping our designs. I am also creading a redesign of the Zara wesbite to improve overall user experience and the user interface. The final project I am working on is a heuristic evaluation of the ASOS mobile application.
